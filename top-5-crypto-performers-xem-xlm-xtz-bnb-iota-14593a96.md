## IOTA/USDT

Iota (IOTA) wants to make the ecosystem easy to use and safe for organizations, large token holders and liquidity providers.

The IOTA/USDT pair has been range bound between $0.244553 and $0.385033 for the past few weeks.

If the price fails to close (UTC time frame) above the range, the consolidation will extend for a few more weeks.

On the other hand, if the price ascends the overhead resistance, it is likely to start a new uptrend that can carry the price to $0.50 and above it to $0.80.
