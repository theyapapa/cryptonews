# Total Market Cap Drops $5 Billion as All Major Coins Take Price Hit ...

###### 2019-01-21 06:01

Monday, Jan. 21: cryptocurrencies are seeing price weakness across the board, with virtually all of the top 100 coins by market cap tipping into the red.Top ten coins have lost between 3 and 7 percent on the day, as data from Coin360 shows.

Market visualization by Coin360

Bitcoin (BTC) is down about 4 percent on the day to press time, slipping from a 24-hour high of ~$3,755 to its current price point around $3,570.

Despite a jagged week of fleeting gains, the top coin has now come virtually full circle on its 7-day chart, and is trading just 0.5 percent down on the week.

Source: CoinMarketCap

Ripple (XRP), currently the largest altcoin by market cap, is down fractionally less, shedding 3.4 percent on the day to trade at ~$0.317 to press time.

Source: CoinMarketCap

Second largest altcoin Ethereum (ETH) is down 5 percent on the day, and is trading at ~$117 to press time.

Total market capitalization of all cryptocurrencies is around $119 billion as of press time, down 4.3 percent on the day, but seeing virtually no change on the week.

7-day chart of the total market capitalization of all cryptocurrencies from CoinMarketCap

United Kingdom-based investor and entrepreneur Alistair Milne has today predicted that the next crypto market bull run will determine which public blockchains become household names in the industry.
