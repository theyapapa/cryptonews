# Tezos Rallies Following Coinbase Announcement, Major Tokens Up Slightly ...

###### 2019-07-30 22:07

Tuesday, July 30 — Top cryptocurrencies Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and XRP are all trading slightly up at press time.

Source: Coin360

Bitcoin has been trading between $9,000 and $10,000 this week, and was showing little action on the day.

Source: Coin360

Top altcoin Ether has had a steady week and is trading around $212 at press time, up 2.26% on the day.

On it’s weekly chart, the coin is up 1.63%

Last week, institutional cryptocurrency exchange San Francisco Open Exchange (SFOX) suggested that Ether may not be an altcoin.

Source: Coin360

XRP has seen a 3% gain on the day to trade at $0.319.

Source: Coin360

Among the top 20 altcoins, Tezos (XTZ), the 16th largest cryptocurrency by market cap, has seen the largest price spike on the day.

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is also seeing notable gains, up over 9% on the day to trade at $330.42, while the only loser among the top 20 coins is Bitfinex’s exchange token Unus Sed Leo (LEO), which is down 1.34% to trade at $1.33.
