# “The Cutest Crypto Game” Celebrates Chinese New Year and Sends Out Digital Gifts ...

###### 2019-02-04 21:02

Decentralized crypto collectible game Blockchain Cuties, which is already available on three different blockchain platforms — Ethereum, EOS and Tron — has announced a celebration of Chinese New Year for its players.

The Blockchain Cuties players won’t know what will be inside their digital packs until the Chinese New Year, on Feb. 5, 2019.

By the Chinese New Year, Blockchain Cuties is also going to present new pets: Pigs, which will join the race of digital animals.

Blockchain Cuties is available here

## Multi-blockchain game

The game developer emphasizes that the Chinese New Year will be celebrated across the whole ecosystem.

The event will be available on the three blockchains supported by the game — Ethereum, EOS and Tron.

The game developers said that Tron’s concept fits the Blockchain Cuties approach: According to the company, the full ownership of the Cuties belongs to the players who brought or breed these non-fungible tokens.

## Crypto-collectible trend

Blockchain Cuties Universe became a part of the boom of crypto-collectible games that started in June 2017, when the the first blockchain game, CryptoPunks, launched.
