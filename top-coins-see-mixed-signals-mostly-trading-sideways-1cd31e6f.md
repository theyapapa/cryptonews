# Top Coins See Mixed Signals, Mostly Trading Sideways ...

###### 2018-11-07 18:11

Market visualization from Coin360

BTC is up 1.3 percent over the last 24 hours, and trading at around $6,527 at press time.

The leading digital currency have seen insignificant volatility during the day, having dropped to as low as $6,441, while the intraday high reached $6,531.

Source: CoinMarketCap ​​​​​​​

Ethereum (ETH) is up 0.16 percent on the day, hovering at around $218 at press time.

Source: CoinMarketCap

XRP has experienced slight losses during the last 24 hours, dropping by 1.35 percent and trading at $0.534 at press time.

Source: CoinMarketCap

Of the top 20 cryptocurrencies, Stellar (XLM), TRON, Ethereum Classic (ETC), and DASH have seen biggest drops at press time by around 2 percent, according to CoinMarketCap.

Source: CoinMarketCap

Meanwhile, an interdepartmental team set up by the governor of the Bank of Israel to “examine the issue of central bank digital currencies,” has recommended that the country’s central bank not issue its own token in the near future.

The Financial Services Commission (FSC) of the Republic of Mauritius has released a draft regulatory framework for crypto custodian services as the country aims to establish regulation for custodian services for digital assets.
