###### 2019-02-24 14:02

## Top Stories This Week

Tesla CEO Elon Musk Compliments Bitcoin’s Structure, Sees Future With No Paper Money

Elon Musk, tech entrepreneur and CEO of Tesla, said this week in an interview that Bitcoin’s (BTC) structure is “quite brilliant.”

New Report Lauds Stablecoins as Key Role in Future Cryptocurrency Adoption

According to a report published by California-based stablecoin startup Reserve, stablecoins are set to play a key role in the mainstream adoption of crypto technologies.

Nova Scotia Judge Appoints Legal Representation for Crypto Exchange QuadrigaCX Users

This week, the Supreme Court of Nova Scotia has appointed Canadian law firms Miller Thomson and Cox & Palmer to represent customers of embattled crypto exchange QuadrigaCX in future legal proceedings.
